Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from prospective institutions

How long does implementation take?

We typically complete full implementation in 1 week. This includes data migration, user setup, staff training, and going live.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade security including JWT authentication, role-based access control, password encryption with bcryptjs, NoSQL injection protection, and rate limiting. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR and international data protection standards.

Can I import existing student and teacher data?

Yes, absolutely. We handle data migration from Excel, CSV, or your existing system. Our team will work with you to ensure clean, accurate data import. We typically map your existing fields to our system and perform data validation before going live.

What about custom requirements?

We can accommodate custom requirements. The system is highly configurable for institution-specific needs. We offer customization services for features beyond the standard package. We'll discuss your needs during the consultation phase.

Do you provide training and support?

Yes. We provide comprehensive training for all roles during implementation. We have dedicated support via email and phone. We also offer video tutorials and a knowledge base. Premium plans include priority support and quarterly training refreshers.

What's the contract term?

It's a one-time payment and this system is yours. No recurring fees, no hidden charges. You own it forever.
